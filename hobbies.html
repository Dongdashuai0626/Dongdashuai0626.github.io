🎯 Hobbies & Interests

Beyond my academic pursuits, I have various interests that keep my life enriching and balanced. Here are some activities I'm passionate about in my spare time:

🏓 Table Tennis

I'm passionate about table tennis and use the backhand long rubber chop style. My idol is Zhu Shihe (朱世赫), the legendary Korean chopper who inspired me to start playing this defensive style. I won silver medals in both men's singles and doubles at the 2024 "Pioneer Cup" tournament.

🎮 Anime & Galgames

I'm an avid fan of anime and galgames. Some of my favorite anime series include JoJo's Bizarre Adventure, Charlotte, and Oshi no Ko. I have a particular fondness for galgames from YuzuSoft, having played countless titles from this developer. If you share similar interests, feel free to reach out—Ciallo～(∠・ω＜)⌒☆

🎵 Music & Performance

I'm passionate about music, especially rock and folk. I performed as the drummer for Boulevard of Broken Dreams during the ZJUI 2024 Freshman Welcome Ceremony. I'm also a big fan of the Taiwanese indie rock band No Party for Cao Dong (草东没有派对).

🤝 Volunteering

During my freshman year, I participated in an online teaching program where I mentored two students from Mengshi Primary School in Yunnan Province, helping them improve academically and broaden their horizons. I have accumulated over 130 hours of volunteer service, believing in giving back to the community.

💪 Fitness & Sports

I maintain a regular fitness and running routine to stay healthy and energized. Feel free to join me for a workout or a jog if you're ever on campus! Regular exercise keeps me physically healthy and provides the energy needed for academic research.
